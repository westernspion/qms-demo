1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
This procedure establishes calibration intervals and methods for the Fusion Draw Machine (FDM) used in display glass production at Apex Glass Technologies. The FDM controls the overflow downdraw process that produces glass ribbon of specified thickness for display applications. Only Level 2 or higher certified operators may adjust calibration parameters. See TR-2024-015 for certification level definitions.

2. CALIBRATION SCHEDULE
2.1 Daily Calibration (before first draw of each production day):
  - Overflow temperature uniformity: verify ±2°C across 500mm span using calibrated traversing thermocouple
  - Pull-down speed calibration: verify against laser encoder output at 3 speeds (1 m/min, 3 m/min, 5 m/min)
  - Glass ribbon thickness measurement: take 5 readings across the ribbon width, confirm Cpk ≥ 1.33
  - Edge trim alignment: verify within ±0.5mm using the optical alignment fixture (Tool ID: FDM-T-004)

2.2 Weekly Calibration:
  - Full thermal profile mapping: 25-point grid across the overflow isopipe
  - Edge bead formation check: bead width 4.5 ± 0.5mm
  - Annealing lehr temperature profile: verify ±5°C at each of 8 zones
  - Ribbon bow measurement: ≤ 0.3mm/m in the pull direction

2.3 Quarterly Calibration (performed by certified metrology technician):
  - Thermocouple replacement (Type S, 1600°C rated) with new certified instruments
  - Full MSA study: Gauge R&R on thickness measurement system. Acceptance: %GRR ≤ 15%
  - Pyrometer calibration: offset correction using primary standard blackbody reference
  - Encoder calibration: velocity accuracy ±0.1% at all production speeds
  - Certificate of calibration issued and retained in the Calibration Management System (CMS)

3. PARAMETERS: OVERFLOW TEMPERATURE
Target: 1215°C ± 8°C at the overflow trough centerline
Pull-down temperature profile: 1215°C → 900°C over 1.2 meters (control by 6 independently adjustable heater zones)
Maximum allowed zone-to-zone temperature differential: 15°C
If differential exceeds 15°C: stop production, alert Engineering, initiate PDR

4. PULL-DOWN SPEED AND RIBBON THICKNESS
Glass ribbon thickness is set by the ratio of glass flow rate (set by overflow temperature and melt viscosity) to pull-down speed.
For 0.7mm target thickness: pull-down speed approximately 2.8 m/min (adjust per actual viscosity calculation)
For 1.1mm target thickness: pull-down speed approximately 1.6 m/min
Thickness uniformity requirement: ≤ ±0.02mm across the full width

5. OPERATOR CERTIFICATION REQUIREMENTS
5.1 Level 1 Certified: May perform daily checks and record results under supervision.
5.2 Level 2 Certified: May perform all calibration adjustments. Must be present for weekly and quarterly calibrations.
5.3 Level 3 Certified: Approves calibration results, signs off on certificates.
5.4 Certification status for all operators maintained in TR-2024-015.
Note: 3 Level 1 operators have training records expiring February 2025. Recertification must be scheduled before expiry.

6. OUT-OF-TOLERANCE RESPONSE
6.1 If any calibration check is out of tolerance:
  a. Record finding in the Calibration Log (Form QF-1095)
  b. Stop FDM production if parameter is safety-critical or product-impacting
  c. Notify Engineering within 1 hour
  d. Do not resume production until root cause is determined and corrective action is implemented
  e. Review all product produced since last successful calibration for potential nonconformance

7. MEASUREMENT SYSTEM ANALYSIS SUMMARY (Last Annual Study — 2024-Q2)
Thickness measurement system (Keyence LS-9006T):
  - %GRR: 8.3% (Acceptable, < 15% threshold)
  - Number of distinct categories (ndc): 8 (Acceptable, ≥ 4 required)
  - Bias: +0.003mm (within tolerance, no correction applied)
