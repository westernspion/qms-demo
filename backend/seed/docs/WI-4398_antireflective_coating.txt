1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE
This Work Instruction covers the physical vapor deposition (PVD) process for single-layer and multi-layer anti-reflective (AR) coatings on glass substrates. AR coatings are applied to display glass, optical windows, and protective covers for Apex Glass Technologies customers. Target reflectance is < 0.5% at 1550nm for telecom-grade coatings.

2. COATING CHAMBER SPECIFICATIONS AND SETUP
Equipment: Leybold A1100-L e-beam PVD system, Chamber volume 1.2 m³
2.1 Base vacuum requirement: ≤ 2 × 10⁻⁶ Torr before deposition begins
2.2 Substrate temperature at deposition: 150°C ± 10°C (measured by IR thermometer, calibrated quarterly)
2.3 Substrate pre-conditioning: 15-minute Ar ion beam clean at 500V, 100mA before deposition (removes native oxide and adsorbed moisture)

3. PROCESS PARAMETERS

3.1 Single-Layer AR Coating (SiO2, Quarter-Wave)
Target wavelength: 1550nm
Layer: SiO2, physical thickness = 266nm ± 5nm (λ/4 optical thickness at n=1.46)
Deposition parameters:
  - E-beam power: 5.5 kW ± 0.3 kW
  - Deposition rate: 0.3 nm/s (monitored by quartz crystal microbalance, QCM-0044)
  - Oxygen partial pressure: 2 × 10⁻⁴ Torr (backfill during deposition)
  - Substrate rotation: 10 RPM (uniform distribution)

3.2 Multi-Layer V-Coat AR (SiO2 / TiO2 / SiO2)
For wideband AR (400–700nm, reflectance < 0.3% avg across band):
  Layer 1 (SiO2, low-n): physical thickness 90nm, rate 0.3 nm/s
  Layer 2 (TiO2, high-n): physical thickness 65nm, rate 0.15 nm/s, add 5 × 10⁻⁴ Torr O2
  Layer 3 (SiO2, low-n): physical thickness 105nm, rate 0.3 nm/s
Total coating time: approximately 45 minutes per batch (12 substrates, 200×200mm each)

4. POST-COAT VERIFICATION

4.1 Ellipsometry (Primary Verification):
Equipment: J.A. Woollam M-2000 spectroscopic ellipsometer
Measurement: Psi and Delta at 3 points per substrate (center, +50mm, −50mm off-center)
Acceptance:
  - Physical thickness within ±3nm of target
  - Refractive index n within ±0.01 of target material value
  - If any substrate fails: quarantine, re-measure, NCR if confirmed

4.2 Reflectance Measurement:
Equipment: Perkin-Elmer Lambda 950 UV/Vis/NIR spectrophotometer
Method: Specular reflectance at 8° angle of incidence
Acceptance:
  - Single-layer AR: R < 0.5% at 1550nm ± 5nm
  - V-coat AR: R < 0.3% average across 400–700nm, no wavelength > 0.8%

4.3 Adhesion (Scotch Tape Test, ASTM D3359):
Frequency: 1 substrate per batch
Acceptance: 5B rating (no coating removal)

4.4 Durability (per customer spec CS-OC-002 §5.3):
  - Abrasion: 0 haze increase after 50 strokes with cheese cloth, 500g load
  - Humidity: 0 visible change after 24h at 85°C / 85% RH

5. CLEANING PREREQUISITE
All substrates must pass cleaning inspection per SOP-2024-028 before coating. Particle count > 5 per cm² at 0.5 μm will result in coating defects. Verify cleanliness certificate on substrate traveler before loading chamber.

6. TRACEABILITY
Batch ID format: AR-YYYY-MMM-NNN. Linked to substrate lot numbers, raw material lots (SiO2 target lot, TiO2 target lot), and operator ID. Retained 10 years.
