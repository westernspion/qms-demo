1. PURPOSE
This document maintains the certification level assignments and expiry tracking for all personnel authorized to operate, calibrate, or supervise the Fusion Draw Machine (FDM) at Apex Glass Technologies. Certification currency is required before any individual may perform the corresponding activities as defined in SOP-2024-019.

2. CERTIFICATION LEVEL DEFINITIONS

Level 1 — Operator Observer
Scope: Observe and record calibration results under direct supervision of a Level 2 or higher certified operator. May perform daily setup checks with supervisor present.
Prerequisites: Completed FDM Operator Orientation Course (8 hours), passed written test (≥ 80%)
Certification period: 12 months
Renewal: Annual, requires re-test or supervised observation sign-off by Level 3

Level 2 — Qualified Operator
Scope: Perform all daily and weekly calibration activities independently. May make calibration adjustments within the parameters specified in SOP-2024-019. May supervise Level 1 operators.
Prerequisites: Level 1 certification held for ≥ 6 months, passed Level 2 practical exam, completed advanced FDM calibration course (16 hours)
Certification period: 24 months
Renewal: Biennial practical assessment by Level 3

Level 3 — Lead Technician / Sign-off Authority
Scope: Performs quarterly calibrations, approves calibration results, signs certificates of calibration. Trains and certifies Level 1 and Level 2 operators. May modify SOP-2024-019 parameters within Engineering-approved ranges.
Prerequisites: Level 2 certification held for ≥ 12 months, 40-hour advanced metrology course (or equivalent external training), annual competency assessment
Certification period: 36 months
Renewal: Triennial assessment by Quality Director + external metrology specialist review

Level 4 — Quality Director Oversight
Scope: Approves and signs off Level 3 certification. Oversees training program. Performs annual program review.
